Bootcamp structure

  • Five weeks of class
  • Two weeks of the capstone
  • Cover the topics we mentioned above
  • Capstone is a “putting it all together” project
  • You get to give evaluations every week!

Class

  • Times below are North American Eastern Time
  • Start at 830AM UTC -500
  • End at 1200PM UTC -500
  • Breaks every hour or so
  • I’m available on Teams before and after class
  • Happy to meet for “office hours” or similar to go over what we’ve covered

Daily structure

The order for this may change a little bit from day to day:

  1. Check-in
  2. Q & A on async content
  3. Discussion on async content
  4. Exercise/Lab/Activity
  5. Wrap-up
    1. What’s the next topic?
    2. Double-check the next day’s async content
    3. Any homework?

Assessments

  • Three assessments
  • Roughly after the second, fourth, and sixth weeks
  • Covers the materials reviewed since the last assessment
    • Plus maybe a few review questions
  • Measures learning process
  • We try to make time for a review of the assessment materials before the actual assessment
